About the Artist

Meet Suhani

Hi, I'm Suhani — a passionate portrait artist with a deep love for bringing faces to life on paper. What started as a personal hobby gradually turned into a calling when people began noticing the emotion and detail in my portraits.

I specialize in realistic hand-drawn pencil sketches — portraits, couples, families, and pets. Every piece is drawn entirely by hand, stroke by stroke, with care and love poured into every detail.

My goal is simple: to turn your most precious photo into a timeless piece of art you'll treasure forever — whether it's a wedding memory, a late grandparent, or your beloved pet.

~ Suhani
